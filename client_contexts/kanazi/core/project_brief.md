## Primary Objective
To provide affordable, community-owned internet connectivity to underserved
households in Bugesera District, designed as public infrastructure rather than
a commercial product, anchored by a Digital Public Library service layer.

## Background
Field research was conducted across 182 survey respondents across two sites:
Kanazi/Nyamata (primary) and Kinigi/Musanze (comparative). Research informed
the project design, stakeholder approach, and M&E framework.

## Technology Stack
- Backhaul: Starlink
- Distribution: Peplink routers
- Access model: Voucher-based payments
- Governance: Community cooperative ownership
- Content layer: Offline-first Digital Public Library

## Framing Principles
This project is mission-driven, not commercially primary. Connectivity is
framed as a public service — a right, not a product. All stakeholder
communications, reports, and community engagement should reflect this framing.
